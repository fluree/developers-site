## Login with Email and Password

Enter your email and password in the login form to sign in to Fluree.
If there is an issue with your login attempt, on-screen prompts will appear to help.

## Password Recovery

If you've forgotten your password, use the [Forgot Password page](https://data.flur.ee/forgot) to begin the password reset process.
Fill out the Reset Password form with the email address associated with your account. Then, click *Proceed to Next Step*.

<FigureWithCaption caption="Forgotten password email input form">
  <div className="flex flex-row justify-around">
    <Image
      img={forgotPassword}
      alt="Forgotten password email input form"
      className="w-full shadow-xl"
    />
  </div>
</FigureWithCaption>

Granted the provided email is in our records, you will receive an email containing a personalized link which will take you to the Password Reset page.
Fill out and submit the form with your new password, and you'll be able to use the [Login page](https://data.flur.ee/login) again to sign into Fluree.
